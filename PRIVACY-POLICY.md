# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 30, 2026**

## Introduction

PolyBot Builder ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## Our Privacy-First Approach

**What Makes Us Different:**
- ✅ We do NOT store your trading API keys
- ✅ We do NOT have access to your funds
- ✅ We do NOT collect your trading history
- ✅ We do NOT track your positions or P&L
- ✅ We minimize data collection to what's necessary

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
**What we collect:**
- Email address
- Username
- Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe, not stored on our servers)

**Why we collect it:**
- To create and manage your account
- To process subscription payments
- To communicate about your account

### 2. Usage Data
**What we collect:**
- Bots you generate (strategy descriptions, generated code)
- Features you use
- Login times and IP addresses
- Device information (browser, OS)

**Why we collect it:**
- To improve the Service
- To provide customer support
- To prevent abuse and fraud

### 3. Platform API Keys
**What we collect:**
- API keys for accessing our free data sources (news, weather)

**Why we collect it:**
- To authenticate your access to platform-provided data feeds
- To enforce rate limits fairly

**What this is NOT:**
- This is NOT your Polymarket API key
- This is NOT any trading-related credential
- This only accesses free, public data sources we provide

### 4. Cookies and Tracking
**What we use:**
- Session cookies (for login)
- Analytics cookies (Google Analytics - optional)
- Functional cookies (to remember preferences)

**You can:**
- Disable cookies in your browser
- Opt out of analytics tracking

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Data
- **User accounts**: Secure cloud database (encrypted at rest)
- **Generated code**: Temporarily during generation, then deleted
- **API keys** (platform only): Encrypted in database
- **Your trading keys**: NEVER stored, you manage them yourself

### Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and logging
- Password hashing (bcrypt)

### What You Should Do
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication (if available)
- Never share your account credentials
- Secure your own API keys in environment variables
- Use password managers

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Data:
- **Active accounts**: For the duration of your subscription
- **Cancelled accounts**: 90 days after cancellation (for recovery)
- **Generated code**: Deleted after export (we don't store it)
- **Usage logs**: 1 year for analytics and debugging
- **Payment records**: As required by law (typically 7 years)

### Data Deletion:
- You can request full data deletion at any time
- We will delete all data within 30 days
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance
